A simple interface that makes taking Goldbacks easier in a retail environment.  It pulls a current UPMA Goldback price and asks for the retail sale total, calculates how many Goldbacks are needed to cover the sale, lets you choose the Goldback denomination to use, and tells you how much change is due.  

• Pulls the official UPMA Goldback price automatically
• Supports Whole, half (.5) and quarter (.25) Goldback denominations
• Supports implied decimal point when entering the sales amount, just hit the numbers and the decimal puts itself in
• Installs as a progressive web app to your mobile device


Simply download index.html to your device and open it with your preferred browser or click the link below.

https://crietmann.github.io/gbretailer/
